My Background

As previously mentioned on the home page, I love coding. It's one of my most intimate passions that ever since I was about 12 years old, I knew that I wanted to do something with computers. It was only when I met a man, who back then was young (probably in his mid twenties), but to me he was just older. He inspired me to become a software engineer, when he told us one day in the backside of our old church that he was a software engineer or was going to school to become a software engineer or something on that order. It was later that I had to grapple myself with the decision to choose between software engineering and computer science. I honestly still don't really know the difference. As you can tell from the writing, one of my second passions is writing. I've mostly had good grades throughout my elementary and high school years. Writing comes natural to me and I love to write, because although I don't speak as well as I write, when I sit down to write, the words almost like to efficiently write themselves. Thus, it's become a beloved part of me.
